Decentralized AI Network

The Decentralized AI Network powers Sentient AI by distributing agents, models, and data sources across multiple blockchains.

This ensures transparency, resilience, and community-driven collaboration, enabling developers and innovators to build modular AI workflows without relying on centralized systems.

Builder Program Launch

The Builder Program Launch empowers developers, researchers, and innovators to create, deploy, and monetize AI agents on the Sentient AI Platform.

It provides tools, SDKs, and resources to design modular AI workflows, integrate data sources, and collaborate with the community.

Participants in the program can contribute to the ecosystem, gain recognition, and earn rewards, fostering an open, collaborative, and rapidly growing decentralized AI network.

Open-Source AI Models

Sentient AI provides fully open-source AI models that are transparent, auditable, and easily extensible.

Developers and researchers can inspect, modify, and improve these models, enabling collaborative innovation across the ecosystem.

By being open and community-driven, these models encourage sharing of knowledge, ensure alignment with ethical principles, and allow anyone to build modular AI solutions without relying on centralized systems.
